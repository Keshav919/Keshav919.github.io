About

Hi, I'm Keshav Rungta!
I am an Electrical Engineering Master's student at the University of California, San Diego.

I am curently a research assistant in the Wireless Communications Systems and Networking Group (WCSNG) run by Prof. Dinesh Bharadia, where I am working on deep learning based object detection architectures using data from cameras and radars. I want to apply this technology in fields like path planning and object tracking. My goal is to build a robust autonomous system that is able to adapt to any surrounding and any environment even in bad weather conditions.

I love to learn about new technology and consumer electronics. I follow YouTubers like MKBHD, UrAvgConsumer, Austin Evans and many more.

I love to play the guitar and sing, read, cook and paint. I play golf and table tennis or listen to music in my free time. I follow Rory McIlroy, Manchester United and Ed Sheeran very closely.
