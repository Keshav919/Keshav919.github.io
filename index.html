About

Hi, I'm Keshav Rungta!
I am an Electrical Engineering student at the University of California, San Diego.
I love to learn about new technology and consumer electronics. I follow YouTubers like EverythingApplePro, MKBHD, UrAvgConsumer, Austin Evans and many more. I've currently been helping Brandon Bosse develop a VR game, based on a book he wrote on Lucid Dreaming, as a part of the UCSDVR Club. I help with the UI and the charaacter's various actions in the game.
I love to play the guitar and sing, cook and paint. I play golf and table tennis or listen to music when I'm not working. I follow Rory McIlroy and Ed Sheeran very closely.
