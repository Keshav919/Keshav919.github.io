About

Hi, I'm Keshav Rungta!
I am an Electrical Engineering student at the University of California, San Diego.

I love to learn about new technology and consumer electronics. I follow YouTubers like EverythingApplePro, MKBHD, UrAvgConsumer, Austin Evans and many more.

I am curently a part of one of the IEEE MicroMouse teams at UCSD where I work on the hardware systems. I am also an undergraduate research assistant in the Video Processing Lab run by Prof. Truong Nguyen as a part of SRIP where I am working on setting up the VR environment for the camera systems. I was helping Brandon Bosse develop a VR game, based on a book he wrote on Lucid Dreaming, as a part of the UCSDVR Club. I helped with the UI, the scene management and the character's various actions in the game.

I love to play the guitar and sing, cook and paint. I play golf and table tennis or listen to music in my free time. I follow Rory McIlroy, Manchester United and Ed Sheeran very closely.
